About pitch sequence data

Boxscores from 2026+ embed pitch-by-pitch data in the play text as a count and sequence code, e.g. (3-2 KBSBFB) . Each letter is one pitch:

  • B — Ball
  • K — Called strike
  • S — Swing and miss
  • F — Foul
  • H — Hit by pitch

K, S, and F are counted as strikes. B and H are not. Games before 2026 have no pitch data — the section hides automatically.

Column abbreviations
  • P/PA — Average pitches per plate appearance
  • Str% — Strike percentage (strikes / total pitches)
  • 1P K% — First pitch strike percentage
  • 1-2P K% - First or Second pitch strike percentage
